Börhör

Description

A delicious and juicy burger recipe that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or skillet.
  3. Toast the burger buns.
  4. Assemble the burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
