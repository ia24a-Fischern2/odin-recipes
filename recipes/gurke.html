GURKE

Description

A simple and refreshing cucumber salad recipe that complements any meal.

Ingredients

  • Fresh cucumbers
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Slice the cucumbers thinly.
  2. Mix vinegar, salt, sugar, and dill to create a dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cucumbers and mix well.
  4. Let it chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve cold.
