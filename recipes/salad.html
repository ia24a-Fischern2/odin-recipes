Salad

Description

A healthy and fresh salad recipe packed with vibrant vegetables and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onions
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables.
  2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for the dressing.
  3. Toss the vegetables with the dressing.
  4. Serve fresh and enjoy!
